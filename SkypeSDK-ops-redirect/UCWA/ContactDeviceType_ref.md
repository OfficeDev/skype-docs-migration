
# ContactDeviceType


_** Applies to: **Skype for Business 2015_

Represents the type of device that the contact is currently using.
            
## Members



|**Name**|**Description**|
|:-----|:-----|
|DeskPhone|A desk phone.|
|Mobile|A smartphone.|
|PC|A desktop or laptop.|
|Unknown|Unknown.|
|Web|A browser.|
