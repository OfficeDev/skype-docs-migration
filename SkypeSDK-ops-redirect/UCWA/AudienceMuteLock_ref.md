
# AudienceMuteLock


_** Applies to: **Skype for Business 2015_

Represents the mute lock status for both audio and video.
            
## Members



|**Name**|**Description**|
|:-----|:-----|
|Disabled|The media is not muted.|
|Enabled|The mute lock is enabled.|
|Unknown|Type of mute status is unknown.|
