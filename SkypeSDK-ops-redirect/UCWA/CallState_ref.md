
# CallState


_** Applies to: **Skype for Business 2015_

Represents the call state.
            
## Members



|**Name**|**Description**|
|:-----|:-----|
|Connected|Connected.|
|Connecting|Connecting.|
|Disconnected|Disconnected.|
|Disconnecting|Call is in the process of being disconnected.|
|Transferring|Transferring a call.|
