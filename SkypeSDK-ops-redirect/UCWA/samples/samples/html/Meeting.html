Schedule a Meeting

Scheduling a Meeting involves selecting participants and setting meeting parameters with the end result being a joinable meeting.

Dependencies

  • GeneralHelper.js (psuedo-namespacing/null and undefined checks)
  • Cache.js
  • Transport.js
  • Batch.js

Tasks

Schedule a Meeting

The meeting task initially needs a list of contacts loaded up to allow choice of meeting participants. After load it is possible to fill in meeting parameters such as: subject, description, announement status, and choose participants. At this point a meeting can be scheduled with this data making a request to online meetings and providing the data. When a meeting is successfully created it can be joined be following a meeting url found in the response data. This is simulated by opening a new window with the meeting url. Update meeting changes the subject and title of the meeting and it is expected that any unchanged meeting data be replayed in the request to change the meeting otherwise it is consider removed. Finally, it is also possible to end a meeting by issuing a delete on the meeting link
